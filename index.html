PHILANTHROPY

At school, I had been heavily involved in work for the community over ten years. In 2009, I was the founder of the University College School Community Action 5-a-side Football Competition. I started off as a one-man team, and progressed to a team of seven people by my last year of school. The competition regularly saw over 250 people involved annually, and raised £1050 in November 2016.

From 2014-16, I served on the University College School Community Action Committee, and was the Chairman in 2016 where I oversaw 31 different events over seven weeks. I led a team of eight people, and we raised over £30,000 for six different local, national, and international charities such as Cancer Research UK, Duchenne Research Fund, Alzheimer's Soceity, amongst others.
